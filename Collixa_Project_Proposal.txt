
================================================================================
PROJECT PROPOSAL: COLLIXA - THE INTENT-BASED COLLABORATION PLATFORM
================================================================================

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Collixa is a next-generation collaboration and skill-exchange marketplace. Unlike 
traditional freelance platforms, Collixa is built on the concept of "Intents"—where 
users declare their goals and the platform's AI engine connects them with the 
perfect partners, tribes, and learning resources to achieve them.

2. CORE PHILOSOPHY
The platform operates on a "Give and Get" model. Users can lead "Tribes" to share 
their expertise or join "Intents" to collaborate on real-world projects, all while 
earning XP, leveling up, and building a verified reputation.

3. KEY FEATURES & FUNCTIONALITY

   A. AI-POWERED INTELLIGENCE (GEMINI AI INTEGRATION)
   --------------------------------------------------
   * Smart Matching: Analyzes user bios, skills, and interests to provide 
     compatibility scores (0-100%) for every intent and user.
   * Personalized Roadmaps: Generates custom 5-step Learning Paths for any 
     user goal, complete with specific tasks, durations, and resources.
   * Unified Recommendations: A consolidated recommendation engine that 
     suggests the top 3 projects and partners for every user upon login.
   * Efficient Caching: Optimized AI usage with a 24-hour cache for scores to 
     conserve API quota and ensure rapid response times.

   B. TRIBE MANAGEMENT (SKILL EXCHANGE)
   ------------------------------------
   * Tribe Creation: Users can list their skills to form a "Tribe" (learning group).
   * Automated Infrastructure: Creating a Tribe automatically generates a 
     permanent Group Chat, meeting links, and a dedicated workspace.
   * Enrollment Flow: Students can request to join Tribes; leaders manage 
     approval and member limits.
   * Peer Learning: Tribes serve as permanent classrooms for knowledge transfer.

   C. INTENT COLLABORATION WORKFLOW
   --------------------------------
   * Intent Posting: Users post "Intents" (projects) with detailed budgets, 
     timelines, and technical requirements.
   * Collaboration Requests: Interested partners send requests; owners 
     review profiles and AI match scores before accepting.
   * Automated Project Groups: Upon acceptance, a project-specific group chat 
     is instantly created for the team.
   * Dual Confirmation Completion: Projects are only marked "Complete" when 
     both parties sign off, triggering credit and XP rewards.

   D. REAL-TIME COMMUNICATION SUITE
   --------------------------------
   * Hybrid Messaging: Supports both Direct Messages (1-on-1) and Group 
     Chats (Intents/Tribes) with real-time updates.
   * Rich Content: Capability to share text, high-res images, and geographic 
     locations (pinned maps).
   * Smart Filtering: Message sidebar allows users to filter conversations 
     by "Intents" or "Tribes" to stay organized.
   * Meeting Scheduler: Built-in tool to propose and schedule meetings 
     directly within the chat interface.

   E. GAMIFICATION & REPUTATION (THE PROGRESSION ENGINE)
   ----------------------------------------------------
   * XP & Leveling: Users earn Experience Points for every action—posting 
     intents, joining tribes, or completing collaborations.
   * Achievement System: Unlockable badges (like "Identity Genesis") recognize 
     milestones and profile completeness.
   * Credit Economy: A virtual credit system used to unlock premium features, 
     boost intents, or perform AI-intensive actions.
   * Tiered Access: User ranks (Novice, Specialist, Expert) unlock higher 
     limits for active intents and tribes.

   F. IDENTITY & SECURITY
   ----------------------
   * Verified Profiles: OTP-based email verification ensures every user is real.
   * Identity Genesis: A unique onboarding flow that rewards users for 
     completing their "Genesis" (core profile data).
   * Role-Based Access: Dedicated permissions for Users, Verified Users, and Admins.

   G. ADMINISTRATIVE OVERLOOK
   --------------------------
   * Admin Dashboard: Comprehensive panel to monitor platform activity, 
     manage user accounts, and moderate content.
   * Credit Management: Tools for admins to award or adjust user credits 
     for support or rewards.

4. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE
* Frontend: Next.js 14 (App Router), Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion.
* Backend: Node.js (Express) with MVC Architecture.
* Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Real-time subscriptions.
* AI: Google Gemini Pro/Flash (Generative AI SDK).
* Security: Supabase Auth (Unified Session Management), OTP Verification.

5. DESIGN AESTHETICS
Collixa features a "Premium Sage" design language—utilizing glassmorphism, 
soft pastel palettes (#E5EEE4), and modern typography (Inter/Serif mix) 
to create a calm, professional, and high-trust environment.
